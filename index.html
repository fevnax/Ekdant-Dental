Our Services

What We Provide

  • Root Canal

    A root canal is a dental procedure to remove infected pulp from inside a tooth, clean and disinfect the area, and seal it to prevent further infection.

  • Teeth Alignment

    Teeth alignment involves orthodontic treatments, like braces or clear aligners, to correct misaligned teeth, improving dental function and aesthetics.

  • Cosmetic Teeth

    Cosmetic teeth procedures, such as veneers, whitening, and bonding, enhance the appearance of teeth, color, shape, and alignment for a more attractive smile.

  • Oral Hygiene

    Oral hygiene involves practices like brushing, flossing, and rinsing to remove plaque, prevent cavities, and maintain healthy teeth and gums.

  • Live Advisory

    A live advisory is real-time guidance provided by experts through online or broadcast platforms to help individuals make informed decisions or take immediate action.

  • Cavity Inspection

    Cavity inspection involves examining teeth for decay or any holes using tools like mirrors, probes, and X-rays to detect and diagnose cavities early.
